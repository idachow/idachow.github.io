Atlantis Hub Display

How do you engage visitors without the ability to interact?

This environmental display is set in the context of a fictional transportation hub. Given three personas - a stakeholder who wants to promote new plane-train-ferry passes, a business traveler with his eye on weather and delays, and a casual traveler interested in being able to plan flexible and elaborate travels for his family, how can a non-interactive dashbaord address it all?

I focused on prioritizing critical in-the-moment information (departures) by giving it the most real estate. I also grounded smaller essentials in the upper bar (time and location). Lastly I wanted the key differentiator of my display to be the PTF (plane-train-ferry) pass motion graphic. By highlighting potential trips and animating the routes, I wanted visitors and travelers to easily envision what a PTF-fueled trip would be like.
