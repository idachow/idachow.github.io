alpha phi omega rush

My role: Rush Chair, Designer

Medium, tools, and skills: Photoshop, social media marketing

When & where: August 2016 - September 2016 (2 months), Carnegie Mellon University, Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania

Shout-out to: My co-chair, Michael Chin, and assistant designers, Marisa Lu & Tiffany Lai.

Summary: I led rush effort for Alpha Phi Omega Kappa Chapter during the fall of 2016. This included design work and social media marketing.

Goal: Appeal to to potential students interested in service and fellowship on campus using a voted-on theme and social media platforms.

Alpha Phi Omega (APhiO) is the national co-ed service fraternity that focuses on increasing and continuing community service efforts on campus and other communities. As Alpha Phi Omega's rush chair, I was in charge of leading the four weeks of the rush process from August to September. One big part of this is creating public relations material for the semester, in order to attract new potential members and maintain a strong on-campus image of our fraternity. Each semester we choose a relatable theme from pop culture, and this semester we chose Pokemon after the recent success of the Pokemon Go mobile game.
