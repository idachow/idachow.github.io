Kart

Kart from Ida Chow on Vimeo.

College students balance tight schedules and the task of grocery shopping

Tasked with identifying a problem space with college students and coming up with a quick, low-fidelity video prototype, we isolated the grocery shopping experience of college students who struggle with getting used to grocery shopping and fitting it into a day of classes, homework, and meetings.

One thing we wanted to highlight is the inexperienced grocery shopper attempting to complete an exhaustive list but having to double back for the milk... and for the tortillas... and for the mangoes. We came up with a low-fidelity prototype of a smartwatch app, Kart, that takes care of grocery list management and guiding you through a store in the most efficient manner.

Context "Designing Human-Centered Software" (Class)
Team Anushka Chaugule, Summer Kitahara
